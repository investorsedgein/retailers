Our Services

Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AEPS)

Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AEPS) is a revolutionary digital payment platform that enables seamless banking transactions using Aadhaar-based authentication. With AEPS, individuals can perform cash withdrawals, balance inquiries, and fund transfers effortlessly without the need for a physical bank card or ATM.

This secure and user-friendly system empowers retailers to offer banking services conveniently, enhancing customer satisfaction and expanding financial inclusion in rural and urban areas. Whether you're assisting a customer with cash withdrawal or helping them check their account balance, AEPS ensures a smooth, hassle-free experience.

Micro ATM (MATM)

Micro ATM (mATM) is a portable device designed to provide essential banking services anytime, anywhere. This smart solution allows customers to perform cash withdrawals, deposits, balance inquiries, and fund transfers using their debit card and PIN. Ideal for areas with limited access to traditional ATMs, mATM ensures financial services are just a swipe away.

For retailers, offering mATM services is an excellent way to attract more customers, boost revenue, and contribute to financial inclusion. Equip your business with a Micro ATM and transform your store into a mini banking hub!
